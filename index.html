Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

Thank you for using AutoCare HQ! This privacy policy explains our practices regarding user data within our application.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We want to assure you that AutoCare HQ does not collect any personally identifiable information from you. This means we do not gather, store, or process any of the following:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your location data (except as needed to provide the "nearby mechanics" feature, and even then, this data is likely handled by a third-party service as outlined below and is not stored by AutoCare HQ)
  • Your device information (beyond what is technically necessary for the app to function)
  • Your usage patterns within the app (we do not track the news articles you read, the maintenance tips you view, etc.)

2. Nearby Mechanics Feature

AutoCare HQ may offer a "nearby mechanics" feature. This feature likely utilizes your device's location services to find mechanics in your vicinity. However, AutoCare HQ itself does not store or retain your precise location data.

This functionality is typically provided through third-party mapping services, and their privacy policies would govern the use of your location data for that specific feature. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any such third-party services you interact with through AutoCare HQ.

3. Third-Party Content and Links

AutoCare HQ provides links to automobile brand websites and may embed YouTube videos. Please be aware that these are third-party platforms, and their privacy policies will apply when you access their content.

AutoCare HQ is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites and services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before interacting with them.

4. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the application. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates. The "Effective Date" at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us at: dev.aswtapp@gmail.com

In summary: AutoCare HQ is designed to provide you with automobile news, maintenance tips, guides, brand information, and a nearby mechanics feature without collecting any of your personal data. The limited use of location services for the nearby mechanics feature is likely handled by third-party services, and we encourage you to review their privacy practices.

Contact our Support Team

Have questions or need assistance? Our dedicated support team is here to help you with any issues or inquiries. Reach out anytime, and we’ll ensure you get the support you deserve.
